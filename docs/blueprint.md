# **App Name**: Stendarr Consulting

## Core Features:

- Informational Sections: Display information about the firm including 'About Us', 'Services', and 'Team'.
- SEO Configuration: Configure SEO meta-data including the title tag, meta description and keywords to increase visibility.
- Contact Form: Allow potential clients to reach out via a contact form.
- Fraud Detection Models: AI models predict fraud using client data as a tool to evaluate risks. The data from completed fraud evaluations are retained for future use by the AI.
- Service Details: Comprehensive description of the services including: Audit & Risks 4.0, Forensic & Fraud Investigation, Cybersecurity & InfoSec, IA & Advanced Analytics, Legal & Compliance and Tech Development.
- Team Presentation: Present team members showcasing their expertise in mathematics, data engineering, cybersecurity, software development, and labor/tax law.
- Clientele Recognition: List main customer segments, with their benefits. These segments are: Fintechs/Neobanks, Tech Startups, Growing SMEs, Financial Institutions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: Dark, desaturated blue (#192A3E), reflecting the deep blue of the logo.
- Primary color: Bright white (#FFFFFF) to ensure maximum contrast and legibility against the dark background.
- Accent color: Cyan (#00FFFF) for interactive elements, call-to-action buttons, and subtle highlights, echoing the light beam from the logo.
- Body and headline font: 'Poppins', a sans-serif font, to maintain the style of Stendarr's logo.
- Use minimalist icons to represent different sections and services, maintaining a clean and modern aesthetic.
- Implement a 'Dark Mode' aesthetic with a focus on minimalism.
- Subtle hover micro-interactions, with the logo's light beam subtly appearing on service blocks.
